.. _lsstcam:

###################
LSST Science Camera
###################

|lsstcam_doi|

The LSST Camera is the world's largest digital camera. It has 3.2 Gigapixels over a 9.6 square degree field of view and six optical filters.

Visit Rubin Observatory's `Instruments <https://rubinobservatory.org/for-scientists/rubin-101/instruments>`_ page for more information about the focal plane, filters, key numbers, and technical references for LSSTCam.
